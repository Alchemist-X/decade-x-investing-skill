# DecadeX Report Writing Template

*The DecadeX (未来十年) report skeleton to reproduce. Fill each section with the corresponding workflow output (SKILL.md / methodology.md). Educational template.*

## Title

`<公司/赛道/资产>：<a contrarian or first-principles one-liner>`
*(DecadeX titles are claims, not labels — e.g. "Coinbase不仅仅是交易所", "英伟达：拿着锤子找钉子的并行计算信仰者", "Google是不是要被全端走？", "日本商社：穿越周期的百年老店".)*

Header line: 未来十年投资学堂 · <category: Crypto / AI / 投资 / 消费> · <period, e.g. 2025年 第X期> · <author> · *Educational — not financial advice.*

## PART 0 — 理解更新 (Understanding Update)

- **Period-lock**: state the report's date and reason from data known then; do NOT impose a later/contrarian/inverted thesis or a self-invented North Star metric. Recover the human's actual conclusion polarity (DecadeX is often *bullish-with-a-number*, not reflexively contrarian).
- If the **title is a question**, lead with it and commit to answering it.
- The single biggest **reframe**, stated as a one-liner (the *human's* non-obvious insight, not your own of the opposite valence).
- If prior versions exist: a V1→Vn table with ✓ (got right) / ✗ (got wrong) per prior conclusion.
- A "理解的变与不变" table (what stayed / what changed): 定位 / 用户画像 / 核心价值 / 不可能三角排序 / 核心竞争力 / 下一个爆发标志.

## PART 1 — 第一性原理 + 品类重定义 (First Principles)

- "这东西到底是什么?" Re-derive the category; reject the lazy analogy; pick the lens (finance not internet; asset not traffic; logic-machine not chatbot).
- Name the **北极星指标** and justify it.
- State the market-timing anchor (资产渗透率 → "1995 internet"; 渗透率>5% trigger; "iPhone1 era").

## PART 2 — 框架展开 (Frameworks, applied with numbers)

For each relevant named framework (from `frameworks-*.md`):
- State the framework in one line.
- **Apply it with numbers** — ratios, shares, correlations, formula-decomposition-then-locate-the-driver.
- Use cross-domain analogy where it sharpens the point.

Typical sub-sections by domain:
- *Crypto:* 不可能三角 (re-ordered) · 协议层 vs 应用层 · REV/GDP 税权 · 价值创造vs捕获 · 流量×流动性 · settlement-stack · Stable/Total Mcap barometer · PS/PE bands.
- *AI:* 逻辑快/便宜/强 · Pre×Post×TTS · ad-revenue decomposition · Core/Gen AI (β/α) · AI+ vs +AI · (compute) Y=M·X / Roofline.
- *Funds/Macro:* 技术革命与金融资本 · 能力圈映射 · 幂数定律/Vintage · LP三诉求 · 渗透率TAM.
- *Consumer:* 硬件→SaaS迁移 (+ self-correction) · 两阶段时点 · 平台vs品牌/船票 · 直播电商=沉浸式广告 · GMV双路径 · 组织架构→渗透率.

## PART 3 — 竞争格局 / 头对头对标 (Competitive Landscape)

- The master comparison table, **anchored to one benchmark** (% of Binance; vs Tesla's historical curve; layer-by-layer vs the leader).
- Distill each competitor to a one-word **核心基因**.
- Separate **value creation from value capture** explicitly.

## PART 4 — 尽调十问 (Ten DD Questions)

- **β:** 行业能否5年翻倍? 十年后行业是什么?
- **α:** 竞争优势 / 用户&产品 / 商业模式(×2) / 运营 / 组织&文化 / 成长潜力(能否≥5x)?
- **Timing:** 为什么市场还没发现这个价值?
- (Optional) scoring weights summing to 100%.

## PART 5 — 估值 (Scenario Valuation)

- Choose **PS vs PE** by token/equity economics (PE-style if ~all revenue buys back token / near-100% margin; else PS).
- **Bear / Base / Bull**, each: driver metric → revenue → multiple → discount (e.g. 监管折价) → market cap.
- Probability weights → expectation. Cross-validate with a 2nd method; take the mean.
- Output a **banded target** tied to explicit *conditional* assumptions.

## PART 6 — 风险 (Risks)

- The **disconfirming / black-swan** risk, named.
- The **bear-market stress test** (does non-subsidized TVL + DAU survive?).
- Organic vs subsidized demand check.

## PART 7 — 核心结论 (House Conclusions)

- A tight numbered list of strong, **conditional, falsifiable** conclusions.
- Bullish-on-X always framed "conditional on (天时/地利/人和)…", with the observable that would falsify it.

## PART 8 — 接下来的工作 / 待解问题 (Open Questions)

- Honestly list what remains unresolved and what would update the thesis (DecadeX ends by exposing open problems, not over-claiming).

---

### Style notes (DecadeX house voice)

- Lead with the reframe, not a definition.
- Anchor on **ratios and shares**, not raw totals; cite **correlation coefficients** as evidence.
- Use **formula → locate the driver** repeatedly.
- **De-frame consensus analogies** ("X 不是 Y, 更像 Z").
- Keep conclusions **conditional and banded**; never a personalized buy/sell call.
- Tag claims **事实 / 推断 / 观点** where precision matters; date-stamp time-sensitive specs.
- Educational framing throughout — distilling DecadeX methodology, not giving advice.
