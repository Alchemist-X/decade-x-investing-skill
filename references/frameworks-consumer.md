## A. Autos / EVs (汽车在线, electric-vehicle)

### 1. 硬件驱动 → 软件即服务(SaaS)驱动 的价值链迁移 — and the self-correction
- **The original master thesis:** the 100-year-unchanged auto industry is being re-architected as software/online/data-driven — the value chain migrates from hardware-driven to **SaaS-driven**, mirroring smartphones (工业化→智能化→消费化).
- **The DecadeX self-correction (important):** by V5-V6 they *falsified* their own thesis — electrification basically complete, intelligence hit a bottleneck, L4 far behind, software subscription never broke out ⇒ conclusion收敛 to **"车还是车" (the car is still a car).** *This is a model case of versioned self-critique: lead with what the prior version got wrong.*

### 2. 两阶段时点判断 (Two-stage timing)
- **Stage 1 = 垄断竞争 / coopetition** (5-10 yrs, "车还是车", an A→B tool); **Stage 2 = 完全垄断** (key differentiated UX + business model emerge, "车不是车").
- Current placement: **"iPhone1 era"** — electrification done, intelligence bottlenecked, the **consumption attribute now dominates**, still Stage 1. Tesla as electrification 品类定义者 is already **price-in / even over-priced**.

### 3. 供给端 vs 需求端 (the "盘子无增量" reframe)
- The change is mostly **supply-side** (production-method change); demand-side has no essential change ⇒ **整个盘子没有增量.** ⇒ counter-intuitive verdict: post-electrification the category is *more* standardized/competitive with **lower gross margin than fuel cars** ⇒ **整车是二流生意 ("四个轮子不是好生意")**.

### 4. 供给侧霸主更迭 (production-method → global hegemony)
- Each production-method shift crowns a new global leader (US mass production → Japan lean → Germany modular → electrification re-architects the chain). A leader also needs a leading *domestic demand* market.
- ⇒ **全球格局大洗牌:** Japan/US/Germany 三足鼎立 overturned; 日系 the biggest loser; "China's supply-demand soil will incubate the next new-energy 'Toyota'" — and **it has probably already appeared (BYD).** Top-15 concentration fell 88.7%→66.5%; 2022H1 BYD 15.4% / Tesla 13.6% top the NEV board.

### 5. 平台路线 vs 品牌路线 + 船票 + 三壁垒
- **Two tracks:** 平台路线 (core = 性价比 / all-in-one integration) vs 品牌路线 (core = 人群/调性/用户心智). High-end品牌 ≈ luxury (优越感/稀有/炫耀); low-end ≈ consumer electronics (functional mobility).
- **船票 (survivor test):** electrification 上半场 over; only **Tesla + BYD** got the ticket. 快鱼吃慢鱼 — grabbing 点位/share now matters most. 蔚小理 = essentially Series-A projects in a secondary-market body; first-mover mind-share may get *capped into niche-leader* status; "有可能出局一家".
- **高端智能汽车三壁垒:** 品牌 / 持续领先的技术 / 成本 — **Tesla is the only new-force maker with all three.**

### 6. 大单品 / 车海战略 (go-to-market)
- Tear open a hole with one extreme **大单品 (big-single-product)**, replicate hits, then blanket all price bands via a tiered **车海 (sea-of-cars)**. BYD: 41% 爆款 ratio, 77% of sales from hits ⇒ captured 10-20万 mainstream pricing power.
- **生产端看效率 / 用户端看产品 (dual-end competition):** efficiency = cost/margin/scale (单车毛利/人均产出); product = ability to make 爆款.

### 7. 渗透率 + 哑铃→纺锤 (penetration + market-shape shift)
- NEV penetration as the master adoption metric (2022: global ~13%, China ~25%; trajectory 4%→14%→30%(2025E)→50%(2030E); 6x-8x headroom). Demand price-band shape moves **哑铃 (dumbbell, hollow middle) → 纺锤 (spindle, fat middle)** as the 10-20万 mainstream band fills in (projected ~50% NEV share in 1-2 yrs).

### 8. Valuation cross-comparison
- **单车估值 = 市值 / 累计交付 (万美元/辆)** + **P/S (TTM)**, benchmarked against **Tesla's own historical curve** (Tesla 46, Rivian 62 outlier, 理想 18, 蔚来 13, 小鹏 8, 丰田/本田 ~2).
- **现金跑道 (cash-runway):** cash ÷ burn (Rivian $15.5B → 7-10 quarters); demand-quality scrutiny (order book, buyer profile).
- **下一个增长点 = 出海 (globalization):** after domestic 白热化内卷, export is the next S-curve (2022H1 NEV exports +130%, 88% to Europe).
- **Contrarian de-framing:** "Tesla 不是苹果 — 更像一百年前的标准石油" (3 possible identities/valuations: automaker / tech / energy-platform); "智能汽车 ≠ 智能手机" (soft dimensions ⇒ landscape MORE fragmented, tolerates MORE brands).

## B. Livestream e-commerce / 视频号 (wechat-channels)

### 1. 直播电商 = 沉浸式广告 (livestream commerce IS advertising)
- **Re-definition:** not "人型聚划算"/团购 — it's a multimodal **immersive ad** (dynamic image + sound + real-time interaction) that triggers 冲动下单; **低价 is NOT the core factor** (random sample: 80% of livestream prices ≈ Taobao/PDD). Information-content ladder: 文字→图片→视频含声音→即时互动 (purchase desire rises).
- ⇒ stronger ad attribute ⇒ **higher take rate** (商家广告费占比 > 佣金占比). Douyin take-rate 11% >> Kuaishou 4% (driven by 推荐占比 80% vs 60%, brand self-broadcast, more consumer-power users).

### 2. 北极星 + GMV 双路径拆解
- **North Star = GMV** (secondary: DAU, 日均时长, 电商渗透率). Decompose two ways:
  - **观看侧/广告口径:** GMV = PV × GPM; PV = DAU × 日均时长 × 单位时间观看视频数 × 直播间加载率.
  - **购买侧/电商口径:** GMV = MAC × ARPU; MAC = MAU × 电商渗透率; ARPU = 客单价 × 月复购频次. (The valuation model is driven by method 2.)

### 3. 推荐 vs 社交流量结构 → 平台特性
- "推荐流量占比 vs 社交流量占比" decides Ad Load, take rate, and 日均时长 (a mutually-constraining set). 抖音 80% 推荐→Ad Load ~10%/take 11%; 快手 60%; 视频号 50/50→take ~5%/Ad Load 2.8%. Platform DNA: 视频号=社交+工具, 抖音=工具, 快手=社区+工具.

### 4. 组织架构/价值观 → 运营力度 → 电商渗透率 (5-why root cause)
- Dig: surface = 运营力度; deeper = **组织架构与价值观.** 视频号's biggest catalyst = 2023 the e-commerce dept moved from WXG/张小龙 to CDG, 1:1-copied Douyin's架构, hired from Douyin ("抖音化"). This is the底层假设 letting MAC/DAU and 月购买频次 ramp in the model.

### 5. 私域 → LTV
- WeChat free traffic ⇒ low CAC (视频号 ~0 vs Douyin 30-40元/人) + 私域 (微信群/企微/小程序) ⇒ high conversion/复购 ⇒ high LTV. Evidence: 周大生复购 30%; 直播预约入口贡献 30%+ PV; 视频号 CPM 43.8元 > 抖音 25-30元.

### 6. 黄峥「人的逻辑」批判 (the closing reframe)
- Tencent's prior e-commerce failed because **社交流量心智 与电商不匹配** ("社交→电商"). But **直播电商本质是广告**, and **"社交→内容→广告" is Tencent's strongest formula** ⇒ 视频号 finally lets Tencent monetize traffic *as e-commerce*. *This is the signature "认同共识再反转" closing move.*

## D. Selected house conclusions (precedent)

- **Autos:** the transformation is irreversible (10+ yr, trillion-$, 6x-8x headroom) but its *essence is electrification*; 盘子无增量, 整车是二流生意, 毛利比燃油车更低. Only Tesla + BYD got the ticket; 日系 will most likely be completely defeated; BYD = the probable "China Toyota" (bullish on its globalization, *doubt* its full-price-band high-end brand); 蔚小理 may lose one; Tesla is price-in/over-priced and "不是苹果, 更像标准石油"; next growth = 出海.
- **Livestream e-commerce:** the category is "更沉浸的广告" not 团购; industry ceiling ~7.3万亿 pre-return (~4.4万亿 post, 28% of网络零售), ~1.5x current ⇒ now存量竞争 (grow by taking traditional e-commerce share). 视频号 is the biggest variable (free WeChat traffic, low CAC, high private-traffic LTV) but skews 中老年; 年轻化 hinges on 看播年轻化→购买年轻化 (gated by 用户心智). Neutral 2028: GMV ~1.1万亿, revenue ~1800亿, ~20% of Tencent.
- **Cross-cutting consumer priors:** apply 技术革命与金融资本 staging + 渗透率 as the master adoption metric + 品类定义者 + bear/stress test of *organic* demand (separate real demand from subsidy/airdrop/刷量).
