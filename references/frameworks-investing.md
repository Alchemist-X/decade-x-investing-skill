## A. 技术革命与金融资本 (Technological Revolutions & Financial Capital — after Carlota Perez)

The master macro framework. Tech revolutions and financial capital co-evolve in **~50-year cycles**, mutually reinforcing.

- **Five waves + AI:** 1771 工业革命 / 1829 蒸汽&铁路 / 1873 钢铁&电力 / 1918 石油、汽车&大规模生产 / 1974 信息技术 / **2022 AI** (inter-revolution gaps 43/45/58/74/40+ yrs).
- **导入期 / 展开期 four-phase cycle:** 导入期 = 爆发期 + 狂热期; **转折 (bubble burst)**; 展开期 = 协同期 + 成熟期. The new paradigm becomes mainstream and the **品类定义者** enjoys the growth dividend.
- **基础设施 → 神器 → 衍生应用 sequence:** value migrates in a fixed order each cycle (infra → the defining "killer product"/神器 → upper-layer derivative apps), each captured in a different phase.
- **Two eruption triggers (require BOTH):** (1) 生产资料生产成本快速下降 (cotton -94%, oil -10x, chip cost collapse); (2) **神器渗透率突破 1-5% (anchor 5%)** ⇒ flips a category from slow to exponential growth. *This is the master "is the revolution real?" filter.*
- **金融资本工具迭代:** 借贷 → 二级股权 → 一二级股权繁荣 — each added instrument lets capital capture more value ⇒ later cycles capture more (神器 value-capture also rises across cycles: Model T +800% synergy; Apple/Microsoft ~600x).
- **断裂 → 再耦合:** financial capital de-couples from production capital (bubble), then re-couples; de-coupled capital prefers **边缘式创新 (edge innovation)** ⇒ **the next 神器 is a NEW company, not the incumbent's extension.**
- **Stage-dependent strategy (延伸思考三):** 导入期 (神器 unclear) = **80% prior-cycle mature application companies + 20% next-cycle infrastructure**; 展开期 (神器 clear) = resolutely back the 品类定义者 + VC the upper-layer apps.
- **Applied to AI today:** still 爆发期前期 (infrastructure sub-stage: 算力/数据/能源, 神器 only a 雏形). Hold Magnificent-7 for certain returns + build next-cycle infra (英伟达 compute, Google/Meta data, fusion/battery energy); watch for the 5% milestone.

## B. 比特经济 vs 原子经济 + 三种效应 (Bit Economy & the Three Effects)

- **Bit vs Atom:** atom-economy value *decreases* with scale (scarcity); bit-economy value *increases* with scale (Mass Adoption). Because software marginal cost ≈ 0 ⇒ supply → ∞ ⇒ monopoly comes from **demand-side effects (反稀缺性)**, not scarcity.
- **三种效应 (ranked for investment): 网络效应 > 规模效应 > 双边效应 > 三无.**
  - 网络效应 = n² (Metcalfe, exponential), producer:consumer ~**1:1** (微信/Facebook), winner-take-all past **Critical Mass ~500M MAU**.
  - 规模效应 = k·n (linear), ~**1:20–1:50** (电商/抖音/亚马逊).
  - 双边效应 = logβ(n) (logarithmic, asymptote), ~**1:50–1:100** (滴滴/美团/贝壳).
  - **Heuristic:** the producer:consumer ratio tells you which effect (wider gap ⇒ more value-poor nodes ⇒ lower ceiling).
- **比特经济最佳模型 (3-circle intersection):** **纯比特生意 + 免费分发 + 交叉收费** (monetize the C-end via a 3rd party, charge the B-end). Subscription/SaaS is *inferior* (forfeits the marginal-cost-0 monopoly path).
- **"运送原子的不是好生意":** bit-purity drives margin (search/social pure-bit high margin vs e-commerce/local-life atom-touching low). Since 2000 the bit economy spread from pure-bit into atoms; **AI and Crypto will repeat the same digital-first-then-atom-adjacent path.**

## C. 能力圈映射 (Circle-of-Competence Mapping) — fund-analysis core

**A fund's returns = the founders' competence circle mapped onto markets.** Where a fund produces the most research = its competence circle = where it performs best.

- **ARK:** research output concentrates in 互联网/区块链/金融科技 = Cathie Wood + team's AllianceBernstein background = best performers (Tesla, Bitcoin, Coinbase). 基因组 + internet-tools = blind spots = worst. ⇒ "ARK = Cathie Wood's competence circle extended to the secondary market."
- **a16z:** edge clusters in 2008-2013 mobile-internet + enterprise-SaaS Vintage Year (Airbnb, Slack, Coinbase, GitHub, Instagram); as that window closed, star deals dried up ⇒ Fund I's 44% IRR was vintage luck, not durable alpha.

## D. Fund-quality frameworks

- **北极星指标 (for a fund/GP): "能否投进核心爆款资产" (can it get INTO the core blockbuster asset).** Winning deals → brand/trust → deal flow → access (a positive flywheel). Surviving cycles = whether a GP can *identify and capture* core blockbuster assets.
- **幂数定律 (power law):** ~6% of deals → ~60% of returns; the best funds don't avoid failure (they pick wrong at the same rate) — they **maximize the multiple on winners** (10x deals = >90% of a top fund's return). VC is a 看赔率 (odds) business.
- **DUAL quantitative deliverable — per-deal table AND fund-family ladder (`data-analysis.md` Part 0 rule 1).** The house's quant edge lives in BOTH:
  - **Per-deal star-case table:** named lead partner, entry round, entry valuation, estimated return multiple — e.g. (genre examples) Uber 49M A-round / 167X, Snapchat 70M A / ~471X, eBay 13.3M / 68X, Twitter 250M C / 56.4X, Grubhub ~55X, Yelp ~10.7X, Instagram 3.57X, Dropbox 2.42X. Name-dropping companies with NO entry-valuation/multiple is the laggard failure mode.
  - **Fund-family IRR/TVPI/DPI/RVPI ladder (every fund, not a "7x band"):** e.g. ~14 funds with per-fund IRR + DPI/RVPI, the single best deal-level anchor (e.g. a fund's book return $12B / ~30x), plus a peer-IRR comparison column (e.g. vs Tiger).
- **ROI-on-capital signature framing.** Add the memorable house stat: **"<$X capital → $Y returns"** (e.g. used <$3B to generate $22.6B). More memorable than IRR and consistently skipped by fund-level-only reconstructions.
- **Portfolio-composition splits:** investment-region / stage / sector (e.g. 56% early-stage VC, 85% US, 76% IT) — hard composition data the laggard omits.
- **VC Vintage Year:** beta + great companies cluster in time windows ⇒ vintage heavily determines returns; VC IRR peaks years 3-5 then declines ⇒ only post-year-5 performance is meaningful.
- **AUM has an optimal band — but the band MOVES with the market (don't 刻舟求剑).** Top-quartile funds historically clustered **$200-800M**; above ~$1B performance degrades and large AUM *deforms the method* (deal sizes balloon, rounds drift later). BUT make the call **data-driven on current deal economics**: if median deal size is up ~8.5x and valuations up ~12.4x since 1990, then a fixed small target (e.g. $5亿) becomes 刻舟求剑 and a new optimal (e.g. $8-10亿) emerges. Treat *fixed-small* size as a **potential liability**, not only a virtue — match the human's directional call rather than setting a generic "red line" the other way.
- **一级是投票器, 二级是称重器:** primary = trend judgment (votes); secondary = quantitative + price (weighs). An "活跃在二级市场的VC" (ARK) bears growth risk without the growth return — can't hold (single-security limits + redemptions). **Buy-and-hold counterfactual** quantifies "拿不住": freeze ARKW's 2017 positions ⇒ IRR >30%, beating its actual churned return and rivaling top VCs.
- **VC value-chain spine: 找得到 / 看得清 / 投得进 / 帮得上** (source / select / close / help) — the mandatory four-stage skeleton for ANY VC/fund study; cover each stage with the firm's *specific* mechanism:
  - **找得到 (sourcing):** the actual channel — e.g. aggressive self-media KOL brand (a16z); **EIR / 驻站企业家项目** (Benchmark-style firms systematized EIR earliest, pay ~2x market, 60%+ of deals via 熟人引荐); a16z's CAA-inspired large relationship network.
  - **看得清 (selection):** the firm's selection *theory* — non-consensus + 结构性积极 vs 短暂性消极 matrix + 洋葱理论 (12-layer Onion risk model); **流动性质量 (Liquidity Quality) + 盖尔定律 (Gall's Law)** = find companies showing astonishing unit-economics/network-effects in a tiny local segment, then expand to adjacent verticals (Thiel "monopolize a tiny segment first, then expand" mental model).
  - **投得进 (closing):** decision speed & access — e.g. 极快决策, 不做DD, 30-minute印合同, 17-day打款; weak 帮得上, hands-off founder relationship.
  - **帮得上 (post-investment):** heavy 帮得上 has **low ROI** — only pays off when a big beta arrives; its real function is brand-marketing/募资 fee. Note the *style* (hands-on vs hands-off). **CRITICAL — do NOT infer post-investment intensity from fund-size/headcount alone (a documented factual inversion).** Some elite small funds are strongly **HANDS-ON / interventionist** (board seats, a "250-hour board rule", recruiting CEOs — e.g. bringing in eBay's Meg Whitman, firing Uber's TK, "对创始人不友好"). Check the NAMED 帮得上 evidence (board control, CEO recruiting/firing) before asserting "hands-off". A confident "hands-off" call that contradicts the report's PART-3 帮得上 section is a core miss.
- **Partner's OWN proprietary scoring models (核心方法论, not generic VC theory).** For any fund study, hunt for the **lead investor's named scoring models** and reproduce them as tables with a worked example — these are the human's core methodology. Examples of the genre: a **marketplace / 十大市场指标 model** (新体验 / 成本优势 / 技术增值 / 供给整合 / 市场规模 / 扩张潜力 / 使用频率 / 支付流程 / 网络效应, e.g. illustrated with Uber); a **10倍估值俱乐部 ten-factor model** (可持续竞争优势 / 网络效应 / 收入可预测 / 高切换成本 / 毛利率 / 边际盈利 / 客户集中度 / 合作伙伴依赖 / 有机增长vs营销驱动增长, e.g. illustrated with LinkedIn).
- **Distinctive PROCESS mechanisms (capture, not just outcomes).** The concrete operating details give a fund report its depth: **EIR program**; **anti-bias decision process** (禁止预售 deals to partners, 不做memo because memos create bias, unstructured Monday meetings, speakerphone with founders); **consensus voting** (e.g. 每人10票, 需全员>5票, weak mandate由发起人定夺); **fee/carry** structure (e.g. 2.5%管理费 / 30% carry平分); **合伙人能力圈分布** hard data (which partner does what % of deals) to show 审美趋同 quantitatively.
- **VERIFY a fund's stated stage philosophy before asserting drift.** Some funds *deliberately do NOT do seed* — the explicit mechanism: **let angels bear PMF risk, enter at the A round at 3-10x after validation, then make meaningful sizeable bets** (an angel-risk-transfer strategy). Reproduce the 3-10x-price / angel-risk-transfer mechanics, not just "post-PMF entry"; do not invert this into "forced down to seed". Penalize speculation that contradicts documented strategy.
- **Partnership-vs-corporate talent-succession taxonomy (deepest org-design depth — a recurring miss).** For a craft GP, compare succession/org models: **合伙人制 vs 公司制** (红杉 / Bond / Tribe / manager-of-manager), the **anti-entropy "self-firing" retirement culture** (e.g. 50-yr-old retirement, hiring in the 30s), "generalist not insider", the **250-hour board apprenticeship**. Equal-partner fragility is only the surface — carry the full succession-model comparison.
- **Quantify 审美趋同 / circle-of-competence CONVERGENCE with per-partner %s.** Don't describe it qualitatively: give per-partner deal-share (e.g. Partner A 37% of 交易, Partner B 54% software-services…) and the aggregate concentration (e.g. 90%+ all in internet, 60-70% in 3 sub-tracks).
- **Post-thesis "has the model ALREADY failed when Beta shifted?" check — use REALIZED evidence, not forward speculation.** Cite the falsified track record: e.g. only ~3 unicorns since 2013 (named: Discord 2013, Confluent 2015, Airtable 2018), and break-out attempts (named: Modern Treasury, Cerebras, DeepL, Hacker One) that underperformed. Concrete misses-as-cost-of-discipline: name the actual passed/lost deals (e.g. Google, Houzz, Slack, Skype; the 2007 globalization failure) rather than inventing plausible-sounding misses.
- **Investment-philosophy is LOAD-BEARING for a craft GP — give it its own PART, not a footnote.** Extract the firm's actual philosophy with its named constructs (e.g. "投资是投人" — back the founder/vision not the track/theme; "不投资趋势 / 自下而上"; Venn-diagram thinking; tactile-reality; complexity-as-adaptive-system). Ground claims in named partner quotes and a source appendix (e.g. PitchBook with date, Cambridge Associates, Preqin).
- **Fund-size "刻舟求剑" with the median-drift math (don't default to "small=good").** Pull median-deal-size and median-valuation CAGR (e.g. median deal +8.5x, median valuation +12.4x 1990-2022) to argue the optimal size has DRIFTED (e.g. to $8-10B), rather than asserting "small is best". The sophisticated house conclusion is "optimal size moved", and a fixed-small target can be a liability.
- **对中国VC / 本土启示 (local-transferability lens) — a recurring DecadeX signature.** Close US fund studies with whether the model is replicable domestically: 生存焦虑 / 商业惯性 / 行业生态 / 文化差异 / 募资内卷 / 地理条件 / LP base. Turns a US case into an actionable domestic thesis. (a16z's biggest miss was *failing to globalize* — missed China mobile internet, the decade's largest Beta.)
- **Hands-on早期 + Hands-off成长期** as the proposed best operating model (via the Tiger-Global contrast and its growth-stage misses) — the prescriptive synthesis.
- **ARK = "基金界的MCN":** Cathie Wood is first a KOL, then a fund manager; core competency = brand力, not return. Marketing funnel: 营销 (fund + analyst IP) → 获客 (social) → 留存 (Inbound). North Star = AUM. Contrast a16z (2B/IP矩阵/LinkedIn) vs ARK (2C散户/single-IP/Twitter).

## E. LP / endowment / family-office frameworks

- **需求的不可能三角: 效率-安全-流动性** (a financial user can't max all three). 效率 = the 敲门砖 (hook); 安全 = a 体验 (feeling, via brand or transparent co-investment); 流动性 = where HNW shift under stress.
- **LP三大核心诉求: Access / Judgment / Protection.** Maps to portfolio: 观察仓 buys Access, 早期 buys Judgment, 成长期 realizes Revenue. Dissect every direct deal by how it got Access, exercised Judgment, secured Protection.
- **MIT endowment archetype:** "**通过投人来投事 (invest in the person to invest in the thing)**" — decide the future growth point first, then find the manager. 自下而上 to pick people, 自上而下 to manage risk. **10%-20%-70%** VC portfolio (观察仓 Access / 早期 Judgment / 成长期 Revenue). Classic LP mix **70% funds + 20% direct + 10% S-funds**.
- **价值投资 / 安全边际:** prefer managers who hold cash over bad assets, use repeatable margin-of-safety strategies, multi-year horizons, analytical/behavioral edge. **四类edge:** Informational / Analytical / Behavioral / Structural (stable LP base → Structural → Behavioral = patience/discipline). "让错误的现值大于0."
- **1+N 决策模式:** 2-4 person deal team + CIO veto/final interview; avoid committee decisions.
- **VC 定性 vs 定量 by maturity:** earlier fund ⇒ more qualitative (Likability, founder attraction, GP驱动力); mature fund ⇒ more quantitative (track-record, check size).

## F. Standard valuation / DD scaffolds (reused across reports)

- **尽调十问 (β / α / Timing):** **β** = 行业增速, 行业规模; **α** = 竞争优势, 用户&产品, 商业模式(×2), 运营, 组织&文化, 成长潜力(能否涨≥5x); **Timing** = 为什么市场还没发现这个价值.
- **三情景概率加权估值:** Bear/Base/Bull → driver → revenue → PS/PE → discount → probability-weight → expectation; cross-validate with a second method; output a band tied to conditional assumptions. (Internet-broker PS 3-5x; defense cash-cow on FCF yield; AI on PE vs comps.)
- **渗透率法 TAM (bottom-up):** sum over each end-scenario's future market size × assumed penetration rate (Veo3: ~8000亿 from e-commerce-live 3.7万亿×1% + gaming-anim 6700亿×30% + digital-human 5600亿×20% + …) ⇒ argues the market is massively underestimated.
- **GPT-3时刻类比 / Scaling Law staging:** map a new tech onto the LLM timeline (GPT2/GPT3/ChatGPT moment) to judge distance from commercialization (Veo3 = video's "GPT3 moment", ~2yr from maturity).
- **军工选股四问 + 估值 (defense cash cow):** Backlog/Revenue ≥2, Book-to-Bill >1, FCF yield ≥4%, no EAC overrun, payout 40-60% & dividend CAGR >8% — *then* valuation (Forward PE < historical).
- **做空筛选 "软柿子捏":** target the easiest AI-disrupted incumbent (slow internal reaction + bad sector + no alpha); the领域 where GenAI ARR rises fastest = most threatened (CHGG -99%). Skip names already fighting back (Adobe/Wix).

## H. Sogo Shosha / conglomerate & cross-border-value-investing frameworks (Japanese trading houses)

The native lens for a 综合商社 / deep-moat conglomerate (infer this before reaching for generic toolkits):

- **COMMIT to a SINGLE focal ticker for the deep-dive (a documented miss — don't stay at the portfolio level).** Pick the company that best illustrates the core thesis (e.g. **三井物产 / Mitsui** as the highest-resource-beta / most-typical capital-killer cash cow) and run ALL the quant on it. Inferring "most likely focal in passing" while keeping the analysis at the five-company portfolio level is the laggard failure mode.
- **五大功能 sequential-evolution (the report's PART 1 backbone):** 商品贸易 → 信息 → 金融信用 → 产业组织 → 事业投资. Each era solves a specific **核心矛盾** and the house **"earns money by eliminating an asymmetry"** (消除资源/信息/资金不对称). Trace the function build-up era by era.
- **Business-history grounding (~half the report) — 2-3 NAMED, QUANTIFIED case studies per moat claim.** The abstract framework is earned with concrete transformations: textile → SPA margin math (利润 80元 → 380元), 成套设备出口 (plant-export), Mitsubishi-Isuzu Thailand auto value-chain, steel-era financing intermediary, information-network examples (Russo-Japanese-war intel). Don't state the function build-up abstractly — ground each with a named, quantified case.
- **Segment-revenue-mix + overseas-revenue % table per company (drives both the resource-beta and FX arguments).** Give each house's segment breakdown (absolute ¥ + %) — e.g. Mitsui resources ~70%; Mitsubishi ¥21.6T, Itochu ¥13.9T, Mitsui ¥14.3T, Sumitomo ¥6.8T, Marubeni ¥9.1T — and an overseas-revenue-% column (4 of 5 houses >45% overseas; Mitsui 54.2%, Itochu only 20.5%).
- **商权 (commercial power / trading rights)** as the organizing concept: the **static moat = "基于商权的综合功能系统"** where the five functions mutually reinforce.
- **Dual moat — 正反馈回路 + 自组织性 (positive-feedback loops + self-organization):** static moat = the function-system feedback loop; **dynamic moat = self-organization rooted in culture/values.** (The deepest insight — use it.)
- **三大变革能力 (three reform capabilities):** 产业分工变革 / 组织结构变革 / 人才体系变革, each resolving a named 矛盾.
- **资本杀手 → 现金牛 (capital killer → cash cow) regime change** via a **4-quadrant capital taxonomy: 发电厂 / 资本瘾君子 / 资本杀手 / 现金牛.** A deep-moat monopoly can still post poor returns when it is a capital-killer (heavy resource capex, ~10% ROE); the load-bearing valuation story is the capex-cycle turn to cash-cow (Capex/CFO falling from ~90% to 20-30%; (分红+回购)/EBITDA ~50%). Classify the business and identify the turn.
- **Game-theory capex discipline for concentrated upstream/resource industries:** prisoner's-dilemma equilibrium to control capex and earn monopoly profit (payoff matrices; Exxon/Occidental cash-cow comparables) — use this instead of a generic "impossible triangle."
- **Government-policy / capital-markets-reform lens:** trace policy intent to its macro goal — e.g. the **伊藤报告 (Ito Report)** / TSE PBR<1 ROE-improvement reform whose true purpose is boosting domestic demand (return cash via dividends, attract savings into equities).
- **Cross-border financing-and-FX mechanism (the most non-obvious insight — surface it explicitly, WITH the bond data).** When a famous investor buys a foreign income asset, reconstruct the actual funding structure with concrete numbers. Buffett's shosha mechanism: **issue YEN BONDS to capture a 2-4% arbitrage spread** vs the 3-5% shosha dividend yield, AND use **yen liabilities to hedge yen-asset FX risk** (liability-currency matching). Cite the bond-issuance amounts/dates (e.g. ¥430B 2019, ¥195.5B 2020, ¥160B 2021, ¥128.5B 2022) and the staged stake increases (initial 5% each → 7.4-8.4%), plus the 1998-Florida "low ROE in Japan" vs 2020/2023 reversal Buffett-quote pair that bookends the regime change.
- **FX is BIDIRECTIONAL (don't treat it one-directionally).** Yen DEPRECIATION is a near-term EARNINGS tailwind for the trading houses themselves (overseas earnings translate up; Japan running a trade deficit) — Buffett's entry benefits from yen weakness — even as it is a translation RISK for an unhedged foreign holder. Separate **operating-FX-benefit from holder-FX-drag.** Treat **USD/JPY FX risk as a co-equal #1 core risk**, not a secondary rate risk.
- **Use EV/EBITDA + dividend-yield HISTORY as the primary valuation/timing anchor (NOT an invented P/B scenario tree).** The house's real evidence is EV/EBITDA and dividend-yield history showing 20-yr-low valuations at the entry — match what the precedent used for this asset class. Do NOT fabricate a probability-weighted P/B scenario tree (0.65/1.1/1.8 → 1.4x expected) that has no counterpart in the report; tool outputs without real financial inputs are illustrative-only (`data-analysis.md` Part 0 rule 5).
- **DuPont TIME-SERIES + cash-cow series (15-20yr, not point estimates — `data-analysis.md` 2.12b).** Chart net-margin, equity-multiplier (e.g. 6.85 → 2.17), and asset-turnover across ~2002-2024, plus the Mitsui (分红+回购)/EBITDA and Capex/CFO full annual series and the dividend-payout-ratio series with yearly values (e.g. 67% → 236% → … → 23%). Anchors: ROE中枢 ~10%, 权益乘数 2-3x, slow 资产周转率, 20-yr revenue CAGR ~9% (→ ~65万亿日元), dividend yield 3-5%, the 2-4% bond/dividend spread.
- **Forward guidance fields (not just trailing):** forward revenue CAGR band (e.g. 5-10%) and sustained dividend-payout-ratio band (e.g. 40-50%) with an explicit horizon (next 5 years), not only a trailing ~9% CAGR. Deliver a concrete **buy-timing verdict** (e.g. "defensive cash-cow hold, but current buy timing is poor due to USD/JPY FX risk"), not just a conditional range.
